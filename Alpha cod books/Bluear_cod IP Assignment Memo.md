# Bluear_cod IP Assignment Memo

## Purpose

This is a practical memo for internal preparation.
It is not a substitute for attorney-drafted assignment paperwork.

## Intended IP Owner

Bluear_cod

## Intended Invention

Alpha programming language, interpreter, runtime, studio, package system, bridge system, and related documentation.

## Assignment Goal

Each inventor who contributed to the invention should assign the invention and related patent rights to `Bluear_cod` if `Bluear_cod` will be the patent owner/applicant.

## Items To Confirm

- legal name of the owner entity
- full inventor names
- each inventor's contribution
- whether any contributor used employer time, employer equipment, or prior contractual obligations
- whether any outside code or third-party IP affects ownership

## Practical Next Steps

1. Confirm whether `Bluear_cod` is a registered legal entity or just a brand name.
2. If it is just a brand name, decide the real legal owner name for filing.
3. Have a patent attorney or attorney-approved template prepare assignment documents.
4. Record the assignment with the USPTO Assignment Center after filing.

## Important Warning

Naming a company in project files does not by itself transfer patent ownership.
Patent ownership depends on inventorship and assignment.
